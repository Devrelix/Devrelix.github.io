GPTPrint Pro
by Devrelix Tech · Workflow & Planning
★★★★★ New release · Version 1.0.1 · Updated Feb 26, 2026

GPTPrint Pro lets you print ChatGPT conversations, export ChatGPT to PDF, and fully customise how your AI chats look when saved or shared. Whether you're a student, developer, teacher, researcher, or professional — this extension gives you complete control over your ChatGPT print layout.

🎨 Theme Control
  • Light & Dark print mode
  • Clean background formatting
  • A4 optimised layout
  • Professional PDF-ready design
🧑‍💻 Message Customization
  • Customise user & assistant message colours
  • Control alignment — left, center, or right
  • Rounded corner radius styling
  • Hide user or assistant messages selectively
📄 Professional Header
  • Add custom title and subtitle
  • Adjustable alignment & font size
  • Perfect for branded PDF exports
💧 Watermark Support
  • Custom watermark text
  • Adjustable opacity and multiple positions
  • Rotation control — ideal for branding & documentation
📊 Advanced Layout Options
  • Page orientation — Portrait or Landscape
  • Custom top and bottom margins
  • Font family and custom font size selection
  • Include conversation metadata
🎯 Perfect For
  • Students printing study notes from ChatGPT
  • Developers documenting AI-generated solutions
  • Teachers sharing AI explanations with students
  • Researchers exporting and archiving conversations
  • Professionals creating client-ready PDF reports
